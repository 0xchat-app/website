Privacy Policy

XChat employs cutting-edge security and end-to-end encryption to deliver private messaging services to users worldwide ("Services"). Your private messages are always encrypted, meaning they can never be shared or viewed by anyone other than yourself and the intended recipients.

Information You Provide

Account Information

When you create a XChat account, you register a private key and a public key. This public key is used to provide our Services to you and other XChat users. Optionally, you may add other information to your account such as a profile name and profile picture.

No Data Collection

XChat does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data, technical data, or usage information. We operate on a zero-data collection principle, ensuring complete privacy for our users.

Messages & Contacts

XChat cannot decrypt or access the content of your private messages and contacts. XChat queues end-to-end encrypted messages on relays for delivery to devices.

Managing Your Information

You can manage your personal information within XChat's application settings. For instance, you can update your profile information or choose to enable additional privacy features.

Updates

We will update this Privacy Policy as needed to ensure it remains current, accurate, and as clear as possible. Your continued use of our Services confirms your acceptance of any updated Privacy Policy.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@0xchat.com
